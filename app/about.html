ABOUT ANDERSON CONSTRUCTION

About Us

For 30 years, Anderson Construction has been building high quality structures. Founded in 1980 by David Anderson, Anderson Construction merged custom carpentry & construction experience to form a quality contracting company. We promise unwavering ethics and quality workmanship.

Member of these reputable organizations:

  • Frederic Area Chamber of Commerce
  • WIB- Wisconsin Independent Businesses Inc.
  • Wisconsin License for General Contractors
  • Minnesota License for General Contractors
  • National Association of Home Builders
