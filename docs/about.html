Who We Are

For 30+ years, Anderson Construction has been building high quality residential and commercial structures. Founded in 1980 by David Anderson, Anderson Construction merged custom carpentry & construction experience to form a quality contracting company. We promise unwavering ethics and quality workmanship.

Member of these reputable organizations

  • Frederic Area Chamber of Commerce
  • WIB- Wisconsin Independent Businesses Inc.
  • Wisconsin License for General Contractors
  • Minnesota License for General Contractors
  • National Association of Home Builders

Meet The Team

David Anderson

President

David is the President of David Anderson Inc. DBA Anderson Construction. He has owned his construction business, including a custom carpentry component for 30 years. Prior to that he owned a custom furniture store in Frederic, Wisconsin.

David grew up in Frederic and has 2 sisters. After graduating from FHS he went on to earn a degree in Construction and Cabinetmaking. Several years later he was presented with an award for “Most Successful” in his class post college. David has built his successful business over the years by continually building his reputation through honesty, integrity and quality work. He has never advertised his company in any media other than signs at job sites and now this website. His business success has come through client satisfaction and “word of mouth” recommendations. David still resides in Frederic with his wife, and has 2 adult children.

Zach Anderson

Zach recently joined Anderson Construction and is the son of David.

Zach grew up in Frederic and has one sister. After graduating from FHS he went on the earned his Mechanical Engineering Degree at UW-Stout. He worked for several years in an engineering job prior to joining the construction team. He is also in the process of starting an affiliated company, “Envision” which specializes in drafting, blueprints, and 3D renderings for Anderson Construction clients.

Mark Ronningen

Mark has been invaluable asset to Anderson Construction. He has been with the company since its inception and has intricate knowledge of all areas of the business.

Mark grew up in Frederic, part of a family of 13 siblings. After graduating from FHS he went on to earn a degree in Construction and Cabinetmaking. Mark still lives in Frederic with his wife. He has 2 adult children and 2 grandchildren.

Steve Wussler

Steve has been with the company for 8 years and was a great addition to the Anderson Construction team as he is knowledgeable in so many areas. He was previously employed by Ford Motors.

Steve grew up in River Falls. Steve recently purchased a home outside of Webster, Wisconsin
